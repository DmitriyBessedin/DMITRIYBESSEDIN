About BRCC Public Foundation

BRCC Public Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to the conservation and protection of birds of prey.

Problem of Raptors on Electric Lines

One of the major problems faced by BRCC Public Foundation is the death of raptors due to collisions with power lines. This is a serious issue and requires immediate attention.

My Experience at BRCC Public Foundation

During my time at BRCC Public Foundation, I had the opportunity to work with the government and other organizations to address the problem of raptor deaths on power lines. I also organized several academic expeditions to study and monitor raptor populations in the area.
